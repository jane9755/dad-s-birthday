A Letter From My Heart

Daddy, there are things words will never fully capture, but I’ll try — just so you know how deeply I value you.

I’ve watched you give up so much for our sake — dreams, comfort, and even dignity at times — just to make sure we had a chance at the best. You’ve borrowed from those who mocked you… and still showed up with love in your eyes. You’ve chosen us, over and over again.

From the very beginning, I’ve been your “delicate one” — with all the health troubles and stress I’ve brought, you never gave up on me. You carried me through the hardest seasons without ever complaining.

Even now, a simple daily call from you can change my whole mood. Just hearing your voice brings me peace. That’s why it really hurts when you don’t call or leave my messages unread. Please Daddy, those small things matter to me more than I can explain.

Thank you for loving Mum so well. Please keep taking care of her — she gave up so much for us all, and she’s a rare gift to this family.

This particular school session has been my toughest yet, and I don’t know what graduation might look like either. But please, even if things don’t turn out exactly how we dreamt — just know I’m giving it my all, and I’ll never stop trying for you and Mum.

I want you both to live long and joyfully, to enjoy every fruit of your sacrifice. That’s my heart’s desire.

And I pray to God that I become so successful that I can spoil you with peace, comfort, and happiness — just like you’ve always tried to do for us.

With all my love,
Your little girl 💖
